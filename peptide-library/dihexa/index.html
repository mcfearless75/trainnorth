Dihexa

Extremely potent HGF / BDNF cognitive enhancer. Researched under SubQ / topical administration, reported half-life long.

Research use only. Not for human consumption. Not medical advice. 18+.

Researched areas of interest

  • Extremely potent HGF / BDNF cognitive enhancer
  • Neurogenesis & synapse formation
  • Memory formation & recall
  • Neuroprotection & post-injury repair

Evidence status

Preclinical only

Strong rodent data (WSU research); no human trials.

Grades are assigned from the published evidence position, not from how widely a compound is discussed. How grading works.

Handling notes

Research-use only. Claimed millions of times more potent than BDNF in vitro, hence very low doses. No human trial data. Long-term effects unknown.

Reconstitution reference

At the common presentation of 10 mg reconstituted with 2 ml of bacteriostatic water, the concentration is 5.0 mg/ml. Draw volumes on a U-100 syringe:
