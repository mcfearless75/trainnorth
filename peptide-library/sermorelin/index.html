Sermorelin

Natural GHRH analogue (29 amino acids). Researched under SubQ administration, reported half-life ~10–20 min.

Research use only. Not for human consumption. Not medical advice. 18+.

Researched areas of interest

  • Natural GHRH analogue (29 amino acids)
  • Pulsatile GH release, mimics physiology
  • Recovery, sleep & body composition
  • Preferred by anti-ageing clinics for milder profile

Evidence status

Approved medicine

FDA-approved (paediatric GHD); widely prescribed off-label in adults.

Grades are assigned from the published evidence position, not from how widely a compound is discussed. How grading works.

Handling notes

Short-acting multi-jab protocol. GH-axis caution; cancer contraindication.

Reconstitution reference

At the common presentation of 15 mg reconstituted with 3 ml of bacteriostatic water, the concentration is 5.0 mg/ml. Draw volumes on a U-100 syringe:
